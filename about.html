From Code to Content : My Story

Welcome, and thanks for exploring my work!


I’m Meghana Kandru, an experienced, Technical Writer with over five years of experience and a strong background in Data Engineering. I specialize in creating clear, impactful documentation that bridges the gap between development teams, stakeholders, and end-users. Throughout my career, I have authored over 100 comprehensive user guides, technical manuals, and training materials that contribute to the success of global projects.


My academic journey includes a Master of Science in Information Systems from Northeastern University and a Bachelor of Technology in Computer Science and Engineering from Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University. In addition to my technical writing expertise, I served as a Teaching Assistant for the Data Management and Database Design course at Northeastern University, where I helped develop course materials, created tutorials, and provided hands-on guidance to students.


My transition from Data Engineering to Technical Writing felt natural, as I was drawn to documenting processes, explaining workflows, and making technical concepts accessible. This passion for clear communication led me to embrace Technical Writing as my true calling.


Being a Technical Writer fulfills me, as it merges my analytical expertise with my passion for storytelling and effective communication. I am passionate about exploring emerging technologies, finding innovative ways to simplify complex concepts, and creating engaging content that enhances user experiences. I thrive on challenges and continuously seek opportunities to refine my craft and contribute meaningfully to the tech industry.
